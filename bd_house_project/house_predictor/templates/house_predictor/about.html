About House Rent Predictor

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate, data-driven rent predictions for properties across Bangladesh, making the rental market more transparent and accessible for everyone.

Technology

We use advanced machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of property listings to generate precise rent estimates based on various factors including location, size, amenities, and more.

Our Data

Our models are trained on comprehensive property data from major cities across Bangladesh, ensuring our predictions reflect current market conditions.

For Everyone

Whether you're a tenant looking for a fair price, a landlord setting rental rates, or a real estate professional needing accurate valuations, our tool is designed for you.

How It Works

1. Input Property Details

Enter your property characteristics including location, size, bedrooms, bathrooms, and amenities.

2. Machine Learning Analysis

Our algorithm processes your inputs against thousands of data points to calculate the optimal rent price.

3. Get Accurate Prediction

Receive an instant rent estimate based on current market trends and comparable properties.
